How do I work with Karim Rashid?

We occasionally look for creative, passionate, talented and enthusiastic people to join our team. We are an equal opportunity employer that encourages individuality. If you'd like to apply for a position with us, please send your resume along with skill sets, computer program knowledge and any examples of work to office@karimrashid.com and note the position you are applying for in the subject line.

Any qualified candidates suited for available positions will be contacted. Please do not send hard copies or original portfolio materials, they will not be returned. Lastly, drop-ins and phone calls are a no-no.

How do I intern with Karim Rashid?

Our internship program is composed of: four solid months of fulltime, unpaid, challenging and intense projects. You’ll be learning in a fast paced environment, multi-disciplinary studio where no two days are ever the same. 

We are currently accepting applications for interns in the following areas:

— Industrial Design (Solidworks and 3D Studio Max are a plus)

— Interior Design (Solidworks and 3D Studio Max a big plus)

— Graphic design

— Public Relations and Administrative

All interested candidates must possess: written and verbal communication skills, organizational and multi-tasking abilities, passion for design, computer skills, and a positive attitude.

To apply, simply send your resume, examples of work, and software knowledge to office@karimrashid.com and list the internship position you would like to apply for. Should your skills match, we will contact you with further details. As with career applicants, please do not send hard copies or original portfolio materials. And lastly, drop-ins and phone calls are a no-no.
